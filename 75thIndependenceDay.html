Breast Feeding Week 2022

Venue: The Breast Feeding Week Programme was held on 04th August 2022, at Anganwadi Center, V. P. Road, Old Madivala, Maruti Nagar, Bangalore - 560029, Karnataka from 2.00 pm to 4.00 pm.

Participants: About 30 women and children, two staff from We Care, the Anganwpadi Teacher and Helper participated in the programme.

Purpose of the Programme

  • To raise awareness on the need and benefit of breast feeding
  • To instill love and respect for teachers in children.
  • To educate the students on the history behind the celebration on 5th September

Highlights of Activities

  • Welcoming of the participants, resource person and animators.
  • Awareness about breast feeding.
  • Clarification of doubts and interaction with participants.
  • Sharing about We Care and We Care Programmes.
  • Tea and snacks provided by the Anganwadi Center

COMMUNITY PROGRAM : BREAST FEEDING WEEK, UNDER ICDS SCHEME

Smt Thresiamma Thomas, (an experienced and trained nurse from NIMHANS, retired Nursing Superintendent of St John's Medical College Hospital) was the resource person. With the help of posters and food items she presented the benefits of breastfeeding both for the mother as well as for the child. She highlighted the nutritious value, building of immunity and other benefits of breast milk for the newly born. She also encouraged the pregnant women and mothers to take nutritious food both during pregnancy and after delivery. She urged the participants to share this knowledge with other women. The participants had many doubts and questions for the resource person, which were answered to their satisfaction. The participants appreciated the programme and expressed their happiness for the knowledge gained.
