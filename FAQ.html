3. Donations & Financial Transparency

  • Q: How are donations used?
    A: Donations directly support all the programs and activities of We Care.
  • Q: Are donations tax-deductible?
    A: Yes, we are tax-exempt under the Income Tax Act 1961.

    A: You can donate via bank transfer, cheque, cash, or donation-in-kind.

    A: Yes, we publish annual financial reports on our website.

    A: 75-80% goes to programs, 20-25% to administrative costs.
