Frequently Asked Questions

1. Donations

  • Q: Who can donate to We Care?
    A: Donations are welcome from Individuals, Business entities and Not-for-profit organizations.
  • Q: How can I donate as an individual citizen of India?
    A: You can make a donation through Cash, Account Payee Cheque or Demand Draft, Bank Transfer, and Digital Transfer.
  • Q: Are donations to We Care eligible for tax credits?
    A: Yes, donations to We Care are eligible for tax benefits under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, subject to the conditions specified in the Act.

2. Documentation

  • Q: What documents are needed to support a donation from an Indian citizen?
    A: To support your donation, provide donation details, personal information (Name, Address, PAN, Aadhaar, Nationality), and a donation letter stating the purpose. If residing abroad, include a copy of a valid Indian passport. Additional documents may be required as per regulations.
  • Q: What documents are required to support a donation from a foreign citizen?
    A: Provide donation details, a letter stating the purpose, and personal or entity information (Name, Address, Contact Details, Nationality). Additional documents may be needed as per regulations.

3. Authorized Collection

  • Q: Does We Care have authorized agents to collect donations?
    A: No, We Care does not have any authorized agents to collect donations.
  • Q: Does We Care accept donations in kind?
    A: Yes, We Care accepts donations in kind. For more information or to discuss the types of contributions we accept, please contact us.
