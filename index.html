We Care is a public charitable trust, registered in Bangalore, India, on 30th January 1999.


We are a secular, non-governmental, non-political and non-religious trust.
Our benefactors and beneficiaries include people without discrimination of religion, race, caste, language, gender or colour. We associate ourselves with individuals and associations of people of goodwill.

Our Programmes

Education

Promoting education and literacy through scholarships, training, and nutrition support, empowering individuals to improve their quality of life.

Disability Support

Supporting and rehabilitating individuals with mental and physical disabilities through community integration, and vocational training.

Healthcare

Providing financial assistance to the underprivileged for medical treatment, hospitalization, and rehabilitation.

Empowerment

Supporting economically and socially disadvantaged groups, especially children, orphans, widows, women, and girls.

Relief

Providing food, clothing, shelter, and rehabilitation support to underprivileged individuals and families, including victims of natural calamities.

Capacity Building

Enhancing skills, knowledge, and capacities through training, counseling, and personality development programs for individuals and groups.
