Education Programmes

Care for Those in Need of Education

Our education initiative supports underprivileged students in schools and colleges by promoting learning across various fields. We provide literacy and vocational training to help individuals secure employment and improve their quality of life.  

Education programmes are implemented through direct engagement with students and families or in collaboration with NGOs and educational institutions. Assistance is granted after a thorough documentation and verification process.

All education programmes are funded by public donations.   To donate, click here.
