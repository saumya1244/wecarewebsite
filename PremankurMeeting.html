15th Annual Gathering of the Premankur Meeting 2022

Venue: The Annual Premankur Volunteers Meeting was held on 07th May 2022 was held at the Function hall at St Thomas Town, Bangalore - 560084, Karnataka.

Participants: About 15 volunteers from Premankur, two representatives from We Care and some friends of Premankur participated in the meeting.

Purpose of the Programme

  • To present the annual report.
  • To celebrate the work achievements of the year
  • To plan for the future
  • To acknowledge contributions of the members and those who support this noble work

Highlights of Activities

  • Welcome and introductory speech
  • Annual activity report for 2021-22
  • Sharing by various members, especially about the past struggles and challenges
  • Party games followed by lunch
