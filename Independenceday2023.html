Independence Day Celebration 2023

Venue: The Independence Day celebration and the inauguration of the Free Evening Tuition class were held on 15th August 2023 at We Care, Pratiksha Nivas, No. 14 (Old 127), 2nd Main Road, 1st Cross, Brindavan Nagar, Chikka Adugodi Extension, Bangalore â€“ 560 029, Karnataka, India.

Participants: A total of 52 people (30 children, 16 women, and 7 men) participated in the program. Attendees included children from the We Care Free Evening Tuition Class, their parents, friends, benefactors, staff, and trustees of We Care.

Purpose of the Programme

  • To celebrate and strengthen patriotic feelings
  • To inculcate love and service to the nation
  • To enhance and affirm the talents of children

Highlights of Activities

  • Lighting of the lamp and a prayer song
  • Cultural program (speeches, dances, skits, etc.)
  • Quiz competition
  • Fun and group games
  • Snacks distribution
