Teachers' Day Celebration 2023

Venue: The Teachers Day was celebrated on 09th September 2023 at We Care, Pratiksha Nivas, No. 14 (Old 127), 2nd Main Road, 1st Cross, Brindavan Nagar, Chikka Adugodi Extension, Bangalore â€“ 560 029, Karnataka, India

Participants: 23 students and 3 Teachers participated in the programme which included Children from We Care Free Evening Tuition Class, Volunteers, staff and Trustee of We Care

Purpose of the Programme

  • To honour Dr. Radhakrishnan and all the teachers.
  • To instill love and respect for teachers in children.
  • To educate the students on the history behind the celebration on 5th September

Highlights of Activities

  • Lighting of the lamp and a prayer song
  • Cultural program (speeches, dances, skits, etc.)
  • Fun and group games for the children
  • Distribution of gifts to children
  • Snacks served to all children
