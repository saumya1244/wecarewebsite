Education Programmes

Care for Those in Need of Education

Our education initiative supports underprivileged students in schools and colleges by promoting learning across various fields. We provide literacy and vocational training to help individuals secure employment and improve their quality of life.  

Education programmes are implemented through direct engagement with students and families or in collaboration with NGOs and educational institutions. Assistance is granted after a thorough documentation and verification process.

All education programmes are funded by public donations.

Healthcare Programmes

Assistance for Those in Need of Health Care

We provide financial assistance for medical treatment and hospitalization, focusing on illnesses such as cancer, diabetes, dialysis, psychiatric care, and rehabilitation for individuals with disabilities caused by Hansen’s disease. Additionally, we support poor individuals and families with general healthcare expenses.  

Support is offered through direct engagement with patients and partnerships with hospitals, which refer needy individuals for assistance.  

All healthcare programmes are funded by public donations.

Relief Programmes

Care for Those in Need of Food, Shelter, and Clothing

We support underprivileged individuals and families by providing food, clean water, clothing, and shelter. Relief efforts also extend to victims of natural disasters such as floods and earthquakes.    

Assistance is provided through direct support or to those who approach us for help. Before aid is given, a verification and documentation process is conducted. We also collaborate and net-work with NGOs and institutions engaged in similar relief efforts. 

All relief and rehabilitation programmes are funded by public donations.

Disability Support Programmes

Care for the Physically and Mentally Challenged

We support and rehabilitate individuals with mental and physical disabilities through community-based integration, awareness, and vocational training. Our goal is to empower individuals from poor backgrounds by providing relief, rehabilitation, and access to essential resources.  

We assist in obtaining medical certificates and accessing government benefits such as scholarships, pensions, and job opportunities. While our primary focus is Bangalore, Karnataka, we also extend support beyond this region.  

All Disability Support programmes are funded by public donations.

Empowerment Programmes

Care for the Weaker Sections of Our Society

We are dedicated to uplifting economically and socially disadvantaged groups, including children, orphans, widows, women, and girls. Our mission is to provide financial support, education, healthcare, and vocational training to empower these vulnerable individuals.  

Individuals and families approach us directly or are identified through our Community-Based Programme. After a thorough assessment, we provide financial aid and necessary support.  

All empowerment programmes are funded by public donations.

Capacity Building Programmes

Care for Those in Need of Capacity Building

We enhance skills, knowledge, and personal development through training, counselling, and personality development programmes. Our goal is to empower individuals and groups to improve their work quality, gain employment, and enhance social and personal skills.  

All training and development programmes are conducted by qualified professionals. By investing in capacity building, we help individuals become self-reliant and empowered.  

All capacity-building initiatives are funded by public donations.
